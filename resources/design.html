Welcome


Welcome to the top page of the Seaquam Design Database!
Please feel free to search up and navigate yourself through the menu on the left.



If you are new to VEX, and if you want to kick off with Design,
our Getting Started page in the Tutorials section will help you a lot!

If you want to know about how to make a specific function, go to the templates page in each of the programming IDE sections.



If you want to know how we think and come up with ideas of code,
go to the Design Process page in the Designing section.


If you want to check and keep up with our new posts and updates here,
go to the What's New? page in the Welcome section.
