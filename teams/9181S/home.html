In the past, I have volunteered by helping the robotics club at Science World as well as judging for STEM competitions around the lower mainland. Team History

Our first season was Tower Takeover as 9181S. We started off the season slowly until we won our first award at the second tournament at Walnut Grove. We won judges for our flip-out deployment. Next at Shawinigan Lake, we won the tournament championship and excellence. In January we attended Port Moody and won tournament finalists and judges. In February we then hosted the Seaquam tournament and won the Amaze Award. At provincials, we got Tournament Finalist, Skills Championship and the Think Award. This was our first season as 9181S.
