Team History

Our team was founded in 2018, where we attended almost all tournaments, including provincials. Then to our surprise, we qualified for worlds! As a first year team this was an incredible achievement, and we knew we had to carry over this energy. Our next year we competed in Tower Takeover, where we were able to improve our design process, as well as our quality of build and code. This year in Change Up, we are planning on competing in multiple online tournaments.
