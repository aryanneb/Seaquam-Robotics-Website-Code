Team History

The licence plate 9181A is one of, if not the oldest Seaquam team plates. Founded with team 9181B, this team licence plate has been active every single year Seaquam competed in the program. Many robotics students start with the 9181A licence plate. That is why there have been so many awards for this team, as this team's licence plate has attended the most competitions in Seaquam history.
