In this section you can find all of our teams accomplishments. This will include all of our trophies and our provincial and worldwide appearences.

Our First Trophy!

Our very first trophy was awarded to 9181B in 2013. They got this award after their amazing performance in the British Colombian December Qualifier. They won the Amaze award which showed their Robots proficeny and innovation. This award was the very first from Seaquam’s total amount of XX awards.
